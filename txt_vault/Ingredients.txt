Pasta Primavera Recipe. Ingredients: tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, garlic, olive oil, pasta.
Instructions: Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add onions and garlic, sauté until fragrant. Add bell peppers and tomatoes, cook until they soften. Cook pasta separately. Toss cooked pasta with the vegetable mixture. Serve hot. Enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookies. Ingredients: butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, chocolate chips.
Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F. Cream together butter, sugar, and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mix well. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking soda. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop dough onto a baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Let cool and enjoy your cookies!

Guacamole. Ingredients: avocados, lime juice, onion, tomatoes, cilantro, salt, pepper.
Instructions: Cut avocados and remove the pit. Mash avocados in a bowl. Add lime juice, chopped onion, diced tomatoes, and chopped cilantro. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything well. Serve with tortilla chips or as a dip for tacos. Delicious!

Mango Salsa. Ingredients: mangoes, red bell pepper, red onion, fresh cilantro, lime juice, salt, jalapeño pepper (optional).
Instructions: Dice the mangoes, red bell pepper, and red onion. Chop fresh cilantro finely. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mixture. Add a finely chopped jalapeño pepper for some heat (optional). Season with salt to taste. Serve with grilled chicken or as a topping for fish tacos.

Spaghetti Carbonara. Ingredients: spaghetti, eggs, Parmesan cheese, pancetta or bacon, garlic, black pepper, salt.
Instructions: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, and freshly ground black pepper. In a skillet, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Toss cooked spaghetti with the pancetta and egg mixture. Season with salt to taste. Serve immediately.

Chicken Alfredo. Ingredients: chicken breasts, fettuccine pasta, heavy cream, butter, garlic, Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper.
Instructions: Season chicken breasts with salt and black pepper. Cook chicken in a skillet with butter until cooked through. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. In a separate skillet, melt butter and sauté minced garlic. Add heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until thickened. Slice cooked chicken and toss with the Alfredo sauce. Serve over cooked pasta.

Greek Salad. Ingredients: cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, fresh oregano, olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, black pepper.
Instructions: Dice cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion. Combine with Kalamata olives and crumbled feta cheese. Sprinkle with fresh oregano leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar. Season with salt and black pepper. Toss and serve as a refreshing salad.

Classic Beef Chili. Ingredients: ground beef, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, onions, garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, black pepper.
Instructions: Brown ground beef in a pot. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cook until softened. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Add kidney beans and diced tomatoes. Simmer for at least 30 minutes. Serve with shredded cheese and sour cream.

Creamy Tomato Soup. Ingredients: tomatoes, onion, garlic, butter, heavy cream, basil, salt, black pepper.
Instructions: Sauté chopped onion and minced garlic in butter until translucent. Add diced tomatoes and simmer until soft. Blend the mixture until smooth. Return to the pot, add heavy cream, chopped basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for a few minutes. Serve hot with crusty bread.
